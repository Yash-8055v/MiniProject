# 🛡️ TruthCrew

**Awareness before amplification.**

TruthCrew is a **frontend-focused, AI-assisted misinformation awareness
platform** designed to help users pause, think, and verify information
before sharing it---especially during sensitive and high-impact
situations.

**रुकें। सोचें। जाँचें।**

---

# 🚀 Overview

In today's fast-moving digital world, misinformation spreads faster than
verified facts. During emergencies or viral moments, unverified news and
AI-generated images can cause panic, confusion, and real-world harm.

**TruthCrew** addresses this problem by providing a **calm, ethical, and
user-centric verification experience**.

The platform **does not claim to determine absolute truth**. Instead, it
assists users by presenting:

- Verification status
- Contextual explanations
- Visual indicators

These elements support **responsible decision‑making before sharing
information online**.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🔍 News Claim Analysis

Users can enter a **news headline or viral message** and receive one of
three assistive outcomes:

- **Verified Information**
- **Misleading Information**
- **Not Yet Verified**

Each outcome includes:

- Clear status badge\
- Calm explanation\
- Simulated search activity\
- Responsible guidance

---

## 🌐 Multi‑Language Support

TruthCrew supports multiple languages for better accessibility:

- **English**
- **हिंदी (Hindi)**
- **मराठी (Marathi)**

Language content appears **only when selected**, keeping the interface
clean and focused.

---

## 🖼️ Image Analysis (AI‑Generated Content Awareness)

Users can upload an image to understand whether it **may be
AI‑generated**.

The system:

- Describes what the image appears to show\
- Highlights common **AI‑generated visual patterns**\
- Responsibly indicates **possible AI generation**\
- Encourages users to **verify before sharing**

---

## 🗺️ Misinformation Spread Visualization

Interactive **India map visualizations** demonstrate how misinformation
spreads geographically.

**Home Page** - Shows overall spread across India

**Analyze Page** - Displays location‑specific discussion intensity

This helps users understand **how misinformation propagates across
regions**.

---

# 🧠 Role of AI

AI is used in an **assistive and explainable manner**, not as an
authority.

TruthCrew demonstrates how AI concepts can:

- Simulate verification workflows\
- Explain misinformation risks and patterns\
- Support **informed human decisions**

AI is used to **assist awareness**, not to make final truth judgments.
